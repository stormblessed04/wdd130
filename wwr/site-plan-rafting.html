Overview

Purpose

To expand the reach of Dry Oar Rafting by giving it an inviting online presence that will encourage visitors to come and experience the joys of white water rafting for themselves.

Audience

Target customers include individuals ages 12 to 50 with an adventurous spirit who enjoy spending time amidst beautiful mountain rivers as well as those searching for a taste of the great outdoors.
