Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this webpage is to increase awareness of the process to join the world of amateur radio. It will shed light on the opportunities provided by the field as well as the specific privileges and licensing requirements for each level of license.

Audience

This webpage has a target audience of people who are looking into how to obtain an amateur radio license. They will primarily be teenagers or adults who value independence and self-reliance that are looking to expand their circle of interaction to include other similar-minded people.
